Food in Ho Chi Minh City overview

HCMC is known for its diverse and delicious food scene, which features a mix of traditional Vietnamese dishes as well as international cuisine. Here are a few popular dishes to try in HCMC:
Pho: This is a traditional Vietnamese noodle soup that is typically served with beef or chicken, herbs, and a side of lime and chili.
Banh mi: This is a Vietnamese sandwich that typically consists of a French baguette filled with various meats, vegetables, and herbs.
Com tam: This is a popular Vietnamese rice dish that is typically served with grilled pork, pickled vegetables, and fish sauce.
Banh xeo: This is a Vietnamese-style crepe that is typically filled with shrimp, pork, and bean sprouts.
In addition to these dishes, HCMC also has a thriving street food scene with many food vendors and markets offering a wide variety of local cuisine. Some popular areas to explore include Ben Thanh Market, Binh Tay Market, and the Nguyen Van Cu Food Street. Overall, if you are a food lover, HCMC is definitely worth a visit!
